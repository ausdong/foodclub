Installation instructions

This platform was originally developed as a blog to view food, but is adaptable for your own uses, such as for your own blog or ecommerce site. To do so, simply download our code from our github repository. Copy it to your own server, and simply edit out the Food Club name and you'll have a blog for your own uses! To download GitHub for Windows click here. To download Django for Windows click here.

Site instructions

To login: Click the Login button at the top right corner. To register: Click the Login button and then click the register link.

To view photo:
Zoom in by simply clicking a photo. This will open a box with the picture. Zoom out by clicking anywhere outside of the photo box. View more info about a photo by clicking the link under the photo while zoomed in.

To upload a photo:
Click the New button in the navigation bar. Fill out the fields and submit.

To comment on a photo:
First, you must login. Click the link for a photo. Scroll to the bottom, where you will see a comment box. Write something and click "Add comment"!
